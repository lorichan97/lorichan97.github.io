My family

Most of my family members live in Hong Kong. I have 2 older brothers and 2 older sisters. One of the brothers live in Macau. The other brother is currently living in Hong Kong but he's moving to Taiwan in a couple of months.

I'm getting married later this year. We were planning to go to Hong Kong during Thanksgiving and celebrate with my family. But now we'll probably go some time next year when the situation in Hong Kong calms down and hopefully this will happen soon.
